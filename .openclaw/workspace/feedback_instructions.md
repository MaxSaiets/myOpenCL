# Feedback Instructions

## Purpose
To ensure continuous improvement and alignment with your expectations, we've established a simple feedback mechanism.

## How to Provide Feedback
You can share feedback at any time using the following format:

```
/feedback <your feedback>
```

Examples:
- `/feedback Good job on the onboarding progress`
- `/feedback Please be more concise in responses`
- `/feedback Suggestion: implement regular status summaries`

## Feedback Handling
- All feedback will be logged in feedback_log.md
- I will acknowledge your feedback and, if needed, ask clarifying questions
- Actionable feedback will be implemented and tracked in relevant task files
- You'll receive updates on how your feedback is being addressed

## Why Feedback Matters
Your input helps me:
- Better understand your preferences
- Improve response quality and relevance
- Prioritize feature development
- Adjust communication style
- Ensure we're working towards your goals

Thank you for helping make this assistant more effective!
